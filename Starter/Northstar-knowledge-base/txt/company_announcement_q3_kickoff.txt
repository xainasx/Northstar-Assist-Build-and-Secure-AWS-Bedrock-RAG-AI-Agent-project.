NORTHSTAR TECHNOLOGIES — INTERNAL COMMUNICATION
Subject: Q3 2023 Company Kickoff
Date: 3 July 2023
From: Sarah Chen, CEO
To: All Employees

Team,

I'm excited to kick off Q3 2023 with some important updates and priorities for the quarter ahead.

Q2 RESULTS SUMMARY
We closed Q2 with strong performance across all business units:
- ARR grew 18% quarter-over-quarter to $42M
- Net retention rate held steady at 112%
- Engineering shipped 3 major platform releases on schedule
- Customer satisfaction (NPS) improved from 62 to 67

Q3 PRIORITIES

1. Platform Reliability
   Our SRE team will be focused on achieving 99.95% uptime across all production services. We've invested in additional observability tooling and hired two senior SREs to support this goal.

2. AI Integration Initiative
   We are launching our internal AI assistant programme, starting with "Northstar Assist" for employee self-service. The AI Security team will lead the security review before any internal rollout. More details to come from the Engineering team.

3. Customer Expansion
   Sales and Customer Success will focus on expanding within our top 50 accounts. The new enterprise tier launching in August will be key to this effort.

4. Talent and Culture
   We're opening 35 new positions across Engineering, Product, and Sales. Referral bonuses have been increased to £3,000 for technical roles and £1,500 for all other positions.

IMPORTANT DATES
- 14 July: Q3 OKR finalisation deadline
- 1 August: Enterprise tier launch
- 15 September: Q3 all-hands and mid-quarter review
- 29 September: Q3 close

OFFICE UPDATES
The London office renovation is complete. Floors 3 and 4 are now open with new collaboration spaces. Bristol office expansion starts in August.

Thank you all for your hard work in Q2. Let's carry that momentum forward.

Best,
Sarah Chen
